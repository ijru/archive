FISAC-IRSF – International Rope Skipping Federation

FISAC-IRSF was founded in 1996 and dissolved in 2019 when it merged with the World Jump Rope Federation

FISAC-IRSF held the following world competitions during it's lifespan

  1. 1997 – Australia
  2. 1999 – St. Louis, USA
  3. 2001 – South Korea (Individual only)
  4. 2002 – Gent, Belgium (Teams only)
  5. 2004 – Gold Coast, Australia
  6. 2006 – Toronto, Canada
  7. 2008 – Cape Town, South Africa
  8. 2010 – Loughborough, United Kingdom
  9. 2012 – Tampa, USA
