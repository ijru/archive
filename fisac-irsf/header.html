FISAC-IRSF — International Rope Skipping Federation

About

FISAC-IRSF was founded in 1996 and dissolved in 2019 when it merged with the World Jump Rope Federation

Web Presence

FISAC-IRSF's redesigned their site 2016, it resided on http://www.fisac-irsf.org The site as it appeared early 2020 has been preserved by IJRU.

An older version of http://www.fisac-irsf.org existed 2011-2015, fragments of which can be found on the Internet Archive.

Around 2004-2011 FISAC-IRSF's site resided on http://irsf.ca fragments of which can be found on the Internet Archive.

FISAC-IRSF's had an website was hosted on http://users.pandora.be/fisac fragments of which can be found on the Internet Archive.
